Park Alloter App

The Challenge

Our group aims at creating a mobile application to help drivers make a satisfying parking decision with the user updatable parking database. Through our user research, we identified that a delay of knowing information like parking fee and parking capacity is our main user pain point.

Target Users

Typically, our application focuses on US citizens on current stage, especially for people who live in big cities where traffic is heavy and find parking locations can be a huge problem. Specifically, (1) Office working class, who are not provided with sufficient parking lots. (2) Shoppers in big cities such as Atlanta. (3) Travellers who on ind it hard to locate a parking lot on a self-drive trip.

Problems Identified

Drivers Leverage on Episodic Memory to Park when they are behind the wheels

Drivers lack of important information to aid their parking decision. Important information mentioned by participants include parking capacity, parking fee, distance to destination, where to pay, etc.

Our Solutions

The segmentation of the targeted groups of users specified in the above section exemplifies why users may want to use our app. In general, the Park Allotter provides users with a solution to promptly locate an available parking lot. Currently there is no application that accurately satisfies such need. The goal of our project is to combine these benefits and functionalities together to best satisfy users’ need.

Design Process

The project lasts 2 mounths with back-and-force desicion makings from the management teams, the product teams, design teams and development teams. I collaborated with a Senior Product Manager, communciated key user insights and delivered ux specifications to him.

03 Design Solutions

Meet Users' Expectations Tightly On the First Page

We assumed that how tightly the first page meet new-registered users' expectation will have a high influence on users' retention rate. New registered users are resources to be transfered into seed users and can be the activators of the snowball effect. We aim to communicate our service and values through right amount of information to avoid disppointing feeling that seed users can feel if they cannot find values quickly in our app.

Parking Recommentations Anchored by Destination

Parking is not users' primary goal, rather it is an inevitable step in approaching to their destinations. Allowing users to view and decide their parking alternatives anchored by the intended destination will create an indication that Park Alloter is professionally help them approaching their goals efficently. Such design will create a trustworthy feeling from our users towards our service.

Points Based Membership

To encourage user reports quantity, we provide a points based memebership plan to encourage users contribution. Parking discounts will be provided both as advertisments for parking lots and rewards to users as a marketing appeal. To ensure high quality of user reports, recommendation and capacity info retrieved will be updated every 15mins from backend, which thereby requires the user reports to be finished in 15 minutes after they arrived.

An Efficent Journey End: Find Parking Entrance

To better help users finish their parking, an important but not urgent feature: parking entrance image is introduced to increase app competitiveness among all the navigation and parking apps. Upon arrival, the entrance image that users updated will show on the screen to guide users until the last minutes.

04 Reflections

Users sometimes compromise accuracy versues time needed.

Trough quick validation and usability testing, we discovered that if there is a way like using memory to find the parking spots, users won't take time to search for someting more accurate. This was a huge challenge that we learned and narrowed down our design scope as well as senarios. This let me aware that we should talk in more depth with the users rather than assume that they would do something as it is accurate.
