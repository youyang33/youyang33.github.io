My Design North Star üåü

I love clean and interesting designs that empowers people. My two design methodologies are Evidence-Based Design and Human-Centered Design.

I follow two diamond design process, which allows me to understand the limits but also do things right. I craft ideas into functional prototypes and communciate the pros and cons of each solution. Besides design, I am also able to do user interviews, surveys, storyboards, affinity diagrams to aid decisions. I also tried to make sure we test our solutions with our end users to validate our assumptions and find out new opportunities.
