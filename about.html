Design North Star üåü

I am pursuing simple, engaging and clean design that can empower people. My design methodologies are Evidence-Based Design and Human-Centered Design.

I follow two diamond design process, which allows me to understand the limits but also do things right. I craft ideas into functional prototypes and communciate the pros and cons of each solution. Besides design, I am also able to do user interviews, surveys, storyboards, affinity diagrams to aid decisions. I try to make sure we test our solutions with our end users to validate our assumptions and find out new opportunities.
