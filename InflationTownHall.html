The Product Request

We want to host a Inflation Town Hall Seminar to demonstrate business use cases to potential clients.
My Contribution

I proposed the end-to-end customer journey to marketing teams to enhance registration experience. I designed, crafted and built the customer registration emails and in-app notifications using Figma and Pardot.

Success Metrics

Through data analytics, we were informed that the event received 216 registration. Over 70% of participants are external users, some of which are previous customers. The SpendingPulse App also saw a huge usage boost afterwards.

Business Impact

This event demonstrated new use cases of SpendingPulse to power users from Merchant and Financial Institues. After this event, I also shared my experience of using Pardot to design emails to over 5 designers tasked with similar requests in other product teams.

Registration Email

Users will first receive a registration email informing them the event, the content and the host.

In App Modal

When users login to SpendingPulse for the first time during the event period, we show an in app model to inform them of the event.

In App Banner

After users close the modal, we display an in-app banner for customers to help them register the event.

Design Process

Step 1: Understand Product Need and Responsibility

After the task was assigned, I listed out our product goal through this project. In order to discuss the responsibility with the marketing team , I crafted a proposed customer journey in order to discuss responsibilities with marketing team.

Step 2: Led touch points design from email to notifications

With clear responsibilities split between our team and marketing team, I started design iterations for the main three touch points. I conducted full feasibility research through exploring the existing patterns. The concepts were shared with the internal design team and product team to find the best way to display the information.

Step 3: Built the email through Pardot

Since this was the first event we sent out from our product side, no one in the team know how to use Pardot. I offered to learn the tool and built the email . After two days of exploration, I succesfully built out the emails and shared the update with PMs. I have also shared step-by-step process to 5+ designers were tasked with similar projects from other orgs.
