Internship at Tencent

From 06/2018-08/2018, I worked as a UX Design Intern in Tencent. Our group works on a teenager social payment product which is called QQ wallet. In the three months internship, I mainly worked on three projcets, QQ Red Pocket, QQ Admin Platform and also several marketing Interface Design.

QQ Admin Platform is a crucial platform for QQ businuess users to manager their social pushes in order to attract new users.
