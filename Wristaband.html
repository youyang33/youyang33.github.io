Wristband design for the diabetics

Won Gold NY Design Award

Based on user interviews, it can take a year for the newly-diagnosed diabetics to know how to eat to keep their glucose level within a safe range, even for those who have diet management resources or glucose monitoring devices. This smart watch is intended to greatly reduce the time for these newly-diagnosed diabetics to form a new eating behavior.
