    Research Outcome

    Target Users

    Small business owners, mainly B2C business, who are expanding their business should be our main target users. Small business owners' main pain points are cashflow management, rising costs, seasonal changes and time management. Mastercard Merchant Insights has the potential to bring outside of four wall info to these SMB owners. We can target at making the platform easy to use, easy to understand, strong ROIs to increase user acceptance criteria.

    Product Value Proposition Adjustments

    Mastercard Merchant Insight Platform has a huge overlap with accounting and cashflow management softwares. While the data looks familiar to the merchants, they find it hard to think of a use case that can be helpful to them. As a result, PM team decided to reframe the product plan and seek out main scaleable use cases.
