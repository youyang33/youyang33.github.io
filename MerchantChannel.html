Intro

Leveraging on its payment network, Mastercard has a special team turning transaction data into economy insights. Clients of Mastercard, typically retailers or banks, can use these insights to make smarter business decisions. In 2024, this team built a new data anlytics tool that speclizes in small merchant business intelligent. The market test in UK was a great success. The platform received huge attention from top payment players in UK. Following Mastercard product design studio process, the platform will soon be launched into United States, Canada, Czech Republic, Brazil, Australia and UAE.

From Sept 2023 to Nov 2023, I led the product design and delivered this dark-theme actionable web platform. The design aligned team's vision through a tangible prototyp and provide answers to a key business question - what should the product look like.

Research Outcome

What goes well

We conducted Design Quality Score testing with 25 participants at 2024. Based on the research, the effectiveness of the platform reached 86 out of 100. 96% of end-users fell they would be effective at helping them better acquire SMBs. In terms of lead management efforts, two-thirds of end users (a combined 64%) believed the platform would be an improvement to their existing solutions and a majority (a combined 84%) believe the tool could fit into their current working flow.

What can be improved

Dark theme has been proved to be effective to some users, while others prefer a lighter theme. "Color combination could be better."

A few respondents are curious about the integration of the solution with their other systems and are suggesting the addtion of some lead performance metrics. "The effectiveness of such data-driven strategies hinges significantly on the seamless exchange of information between systems."

Project Sucess

New Partnerships

Within a few weeks of release, the product is already receiving attention from top Acquirers, POS/ISV and SMB focused corporates. Players like Elevon, Square and Worldpay are using it in the UK to extract key insights into where and who high-value SMB leads are. The platform is being deployed in the 7 countries and 4 regions now, and more countries will follow.
