Intro

Mastercard Channel Insights is a new financial platform targeting at sales people at first-tier banks. I was responsible for the innovative version of the concept, which was chosen from 3 alternatives to enter the UK and Brazil market in 2024 Q1.

Its simple flows, high contrast and low tech requirements turn ambiguity into clarity for the whole team. Demoing the concept to clients, the product team wins new deals with PayFac and Banks.
